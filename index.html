WELCOME

TO THE BAY

BAR & GRILL

_____________

We are an independent bar and grill located in the picturesque ancient village of Lindos, near The Famours Acropolis. We serve quality, premium products in a traditional, grecian environment.

Short walk to the beautiful sapphire waters, with a shortcut direct from The Bay Bar & Grill. Pets are welcome inside our pub in the main bar area. Treats and water bowls are available. Please don't mind the local cats!

During the summer months, we host barbeque parties in our courtyard complete with its own outside bar.

Fresh food is served Tuesday to Sunday with many options to suit all.
